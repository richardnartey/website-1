About Us

Brief History

Divine Deseret Preparatory Schoool formerly called Deseret International School, was established in the year 2003, September 23. The school began with only 31 number of pupils from Kindergarten to Primary 4, with 8 teachers.

Divine Deseret Preparatory Schoool sat for her first external examination (i.e. Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE)) in the year 2009 with 23 candidates who emerged with flying colours. The candidates obtained best of grades raging from aggregate 6 (8 ones) to the least aggregate of 22.

Pioneers of the school have been successful with their lives aftermath. The school has natured a lot of professionals in some vital occupations such as law, medicine, engineering, teaching, nursing, etc.

The School still intends to producing many better products to lead our dear country Ghana.

Students who continue with their second cycle of education (SHS) have no worries of the inevitable challenges they may face at the second cycle; because of the solid foundations of learning they gained at Divine Deseret Preparatory School.
